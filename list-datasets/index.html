    Below is a list of all the datasets included in this ortext. When you are browsing a specific section, the data link in the navigation bar will take you to statistics and datasets in that section.

    Eastern State Penitentiary Costs, 1821 to 1844

    Construction and operating cost of the Eastern State Penitentiary near Philadelphia, which pioneered the separate system of suppressing prisoners' communication. Cost per cell compared to other early U.S. prisons.
    Dataset id: eastern-state-penitentiary-costs

    Prisoner Communication Policies in Mid-19th-Century U.S.

    Communications policies in individual U.S. state prisons and some local houses of correction and workshops about 1870. Communication policies in five New York county prisons in 1854.
    Dataset id: prisoner-communication-mid-19th-century

    Spectator Business at Early 19th-Century U.S. Prisons

    Spectator counts and spectator revenue for U.S. state prisons in the 1840s, with time series 1820 to 1862 for Auburn State Prison, New York; and 1829 to 1902 for Eastern State Penitentiary, Pennsylvania.
    Dataset id: prisoners-spectators

    Prisoner Deaths in U.S. State Prisons, 1820 to 1843

    Prisoner deaths, prison populations, and death rates yearly for a cross-section of U.S. state prisons, 1820 to 1843. Some data on race and sex. Comparison to over-all U.S. population.
    Dataset id: prisoners-us-population-deaths-1820-43

    Parole Difficulties Related to Prisoners’ Communication

    Cross-tabulation of parole difficulties and prisoners' communication with family and friends (written correspondence and visits) for prisoners in the California Dept. Corrections about 1970.
    Dataset id: parole-prisoners-communication

    John Howard’s First Visits to County Jails

    Prison name, county, date of visit, and debtors and felons present according to John Howard's prison visits prior to his first testimony to House of Commons, March 4, 1774, as well as Howard's first visits to prisons in other English counties.
    Dataset id: howard-prison-visits

    Prisoners Per Cell in U.S. State Prisons, 1868-1873

    Number of cells and average number of prisoners for individual U.S. state prisons (and some large houses of correction and workhouses) in 1868 and 1873. Calculated prisoners per cell.
    Dataset id: state-prisons-cells-prisoners-1868-1873

    Eastern State Penitentiary Prisoner Population, 1829-1903

    Prisoners received, average number, number discharged, number present on Dec. 31 in Eastern State Penitentiary, by year, 1829 to 1903.
    Dataset id: eastern-state-prisoner-population

    Visits to Prisoners in Eastern State Penitentiary, 1846-1903

    Visits per year and per prisoner by members of the Philadelphia Society for Alleviating the Miseries of Public Prisons, 1846-1903, with some per visitor data. Visits to prisoners by moral instructor employed by Eastern State Penitentiary.
    Dataset id: eastern-state-official-visitors

    Prisoner Communication Policies Early in the 20th Century

    Communication policies for prisoners (visiting prisoners and prisoners writing and receiving letters) in U.S. states prisons and in some other countries' prisons in 1902 and 1912.
    Dataset id: prisoner-communication-early-20th-century

    Prison Communication Policies Late in the 20th Century

    Writing, visiting, and telephoning regulations by state for prisoners in U.S. state prisons from 1958 to 2005.
    Dataset id: prisoner-communication-late-20th-century

    Prisoners Per Sleeping Space in U.S. Prisons and Jails, 1997

    Prisoners per sleeping space# in U.S. federal and state prisons in 1997, by prison architecture, weighted by prison populations. Similar statistics for U.S. local jails in 1996.
    Dataset id: prisoners-interaction

    Share of Adults with Voting Rights, U.S. & Britain, 1790 & 1930

    Shares of the adult population with voting rights, 1790, 1910-1930, for the United States and Britain. Includes statistics on disenfranchised felons in the U.S. in 2004.
    Dataset id: electoral-franchise

    Criminal Justice Within the U.S. Federal Jurisdiction

    Sex ratios of persons arrested, committed to prison, and in prison within the U.S. federal criminal justice system from 1998 to 2011. Data on suspects and cases closed by offense, but not by sex.
    Dataset id: criminal-justice-us-federal

    Shares of UK Men in Military Service During Major Wars

    Counts and shares of men in United Kingdom armed forces from 1787-1816 (mobilization for Napoleonic wars) and 1909-1949 (mobilization for World War I & II).
    Dataset id: military-service-uk

    Punishment Prevalence Yearly by Sex in England and Wales from 1715

    Prisoners, banishments (transportation), and executions yearly by sex in England & Wales from 1715, aggregated to punishment per 100,000 of population (punishment prevalence), with sex ratio of punishment and punishment distribution (% absent from execution, % absent from being transported, and % absent from being in prison).
    Dataset id: punishment-england-wales-long-run

    Punishment Sentences by Sex at Old Bailey, 1674-1913

    Punishment sentences by category and subcategory, yearly by sex, for cases in the Old Bailey court, London, from 1674-1913. Counts are of persons sentenced to specified punishments.
    Dataset id: punishment-england-wales-old-bailey

    Executions Yearly by Sex in England and Wales, 1715-1799

    Penal executions in England and Wales before 1800, by year and by sex. Includes data by region.
    Dataset id: executions-england-wales-before-1800

    Executions by Year and Sex about London Before 1840

    Persons executed under death penalty, by year and by sex in London area (London and Middlesex). Includes data on sentences, reprieves, and execution type (hanging and burning).
    Dataset id: executions-england-wales-london-pre-1840

    Executions Yearly by Sex in England and Wales from 1800

    Persons executed, by year and by sex in England and Wales from 1800. Year summary counts and person-specific data indicating place and date of execution and age of person.
    Dataset id: executions-england-wales-from-1800

    Convicts Transported to American from England and Wales, 1716-1775

    Convicts transported to America, 1716-1775, totals by year and sex. Convicts transported from London, Middlesex, and the Home Counties by voyage, totaled and scaled up to England and Wales and separated by sex.
    Dataset id: transported-england-wales-america

    British Convicts Transported to Australia, Annually by Sex, 1787-1867

    British convicts transported to Australia, annually by sex, 1787-1867. Roster of transportation voyages, including ship name, ship characteristics, departure and arrival dates, departure place, destination, embarked convicts, and landed convicts. With data on transported convicts ages and length of transportation sentences.
    Dataset id: transported-uk-australia

    Prisoners in England and Wales from 1670: Long-run Trends

    Long-run data on prisoner counts by sex. Prisoners distinguished as debtors and criminals, prisoners held in local prisons and convict prisons, including hulks. Yearly data on prison commitments by sex, 1905-1892.
    Dataset id: prisoners-england-wales-long-run

    Estimates of Debtor-Prisoners in England Before 1775

    Contemporary claims before 1775 about the number of prisoners for debt, ranging from 5,000 to over 100,000. Daniel Defoe's figures for debtor prisoners about 1709. Debtors counts by prison, 1691 and 1779.
    Dataset id: prisoners-england-wales-debtors-pre-1775

    Prisoners in England and Wales about 1780

    Prisoner statistics from John Howard's prison inspections, 1776-1788, including prisoner counts for individual prisons in 1779.
    Dataset id: prisoners-england-wales-1780

    Prisoners in England and Wales in 1800

    Total prisoners by sex and by debtor / non-debtor, roughly estimated using commitment trends, hulk statistics, and prisoner totals for 1810.
    Dataset id: prisoners-england-wales-1800

    Imprisoned Debtors in England about 1800

    Prison populations of debtor-prisoners, commitments, sex ratio of imprisoned debtors, familial relations of imprisoned debtors, and the legal process of debtor incarceration.
    Dataset id: prisoners-england-wales-debtors-1800

    Criminal Offenders Committed for Trial in England and Wales, 1805-06

    Dataset id: prisoners-england-wales-commitments-1805-6

    Prisoners in England and Wales in 1810

    Prisoners totals by individual prisons, by sex and debtors, felons, and petty offenders, with prisoners in hulks and in military prisons, from James Neild's State of the Prisons (1812).
    Dataset id: prisoners-england-wales-1810

    Prisoners in England and Wales in 1820

    Prisoner for individual prisons from prison inspectors' Gaol Returns for 1820. Estimated total prisoners, accounting for misreporting of commitments as prisoners in prison.
    Dataset id: prisoners-england-wales-1820

    Prisoners in England and Wales in 1823

    Gaol Returns for England and Wales1823, showing debtors, misdemeanors, and felons by sex, capacity in separate cells and total, commitments, and greatest number held. With calculated summary statistics.
    Dataset id: prisoners-england-wales-1823

    Prisoners in England and Wales in 1830

    Debtors, criminals, and prisoners in hulks by sex for individual prisons in England & Wales in 1830. Commitments, untried prisoners, greatest number of prisoners at one time. From prison inspectors reports.
    Dataset id: prisoners-england-wales-1830

    Prisoners in England and Wales in 1836

    Prisoners counts for individual prisons in England and Wales in 1836. For each prison, county, place, institution type, in custody by sex, and received (commitments) by sex. Estimated total for England and Wales.
    Dataset id: prisoners-england-wales-1836

    Prisoners in England and Wales in 1838

    Average number of prisoners in 1838, by sex for individual prisons, with estimated total for England and Wales. From prison inspector reports.
    Dataset id: prisoners-england-wales-1838

    Prisoners in England and Wales in 1840

    Average number of prisoners by sex, greatest number of prisoners by sex, for individual prisons in England and Wales in 1840, with estimated prisoners totals for England and Wales. From prison inspectors' reports.
    Dataset id: prisoners-england-wales-1840

    Prisoners in England and Wales in 1850

    Average number of prisoners by sex for individual prisons in 1850, with estimated totals for England and Wales. From prison inspectors' reports.
    Dataset id: prisoners-england-wales-1850

    Prisoners in England and Wales in 1864

    Dataset id: prisoners-england-wales-1864

    Prisoners in England and Wales in 1892

    Dataset id: prisoners-england-wales-1892

    Prisoners in England and Wales by County, 1780-1850

    Prisoners in custody summed from individual prison reports of prisoner inspectors to county-level totals, by decade, excluding 1790 and 1800. Totals exclude prisoners held in hulks. Matching prisons across years in prison inspectors' reports is difficult because of vague and inconsistent prison descriptions.
    Dataset id: prisoners-england-wales-counties-1780-1850

    Prisoners Held on Hulks in England, 1777 to 1856

    Yearly figures for convict prisoners held on hulks, 1777-1856, with ship-specific data by year compared for Campbell (2001) and original hulk inspector reports.
    Dataset id: prisoners-england-wales-hulks

    Prisoners in London Prisons, Summary Data 1779-1850

    Dataset id: prisoners-england-wales-london-1779-1850

    Prison-Specific Data for Major London Prisons Before 1850
