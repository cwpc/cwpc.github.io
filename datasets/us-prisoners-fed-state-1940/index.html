II. Gender Imprisonment A. Punishment Sex Ratio 3. Punishment Trends

Prisoners in U.S. State and Federal Prisons, 1940


Prisoners by sex and by state, reconciled across national Census of 1940 and Bureau of Justice Statistics’ annual series of prisoners in state and federal prisons. Consistent treatment of juvenile delinquents makes the figures comparable.

Get dataset in form/version:
  • Google Sheet published to web (fast, resilient)
  • Microsoft Excel workbook (primary version)
  • Apache OpenOffice Calc spreadsheet
  • Google Sheet (downloadable)
  • Microsoft Excel workbook (secondary, use only if above fail)

Dataset sheets:

  • fed-state: Bureau of Justice Statistics’ annual series of prisoners in state and federal prisons, by prison, for 1938-40. Prisoners by sex only for 1938.
  • census: prisoners by age, sex, and state from the national Census of 1940
  • matched: state-level prisoner totals matched across Bureau of Justice series and Census of 1940

Related datsets:

  • prisoners in the U.S. from 1900
  • prisoners by state and sex, U.S. 1880-2010
  • imprisonment prevalence and sex ratio in U.S. from 1850
