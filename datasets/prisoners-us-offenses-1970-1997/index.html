Prisoners Held for Drug Offense, US 1970-1997


Prisoners by offense and by state and federal prisons from 1970 to 1997, with calculated drug offense prisoner shares. Doesn’t encompass jails.

Get dataset in form/version:
  • Google Sheet published to web (fast, resilient)
  • Microsoft Excel workbook (primary version)
  • Apache OpenOffice Calc spreadsheet
  • Google Sheet (downloadable)
  • Microsoft Excel workbook (secondary, use only if above fail)

Dataset sheets:

  • drug offense share: prisoners, by state and federal, held for drug offenses, yearly 1970-1997
  • state prisoners by offense: source data for calculated drug offense share
  • federal prisoners by offense: source data for calculated drug offense share

Related datasets:

  • prisoners by state and sex, U.S. 1880-2010
  • imprisonment prevalence and sex ratio in U.S. from 1850
  • criminal justice within the U.S. federal jurisdiction
