I. Communicative Walls F. Knowledge of Prisoners 1. John Howard

John Howard’s First Visits to County Jails


Prison name, county, date of visit, and debtors and felons present according to John Howard’s prison visits prior to his first testimony to House of Commons, March 4, 1774, as well as Howard’s first visits to prisons in other English counties.

Get dataset in form/version:
  • Google Sheet published to web (fast, resilient)
  • Microsoft Excel workbook (primary version)
  • Apache OpenOffice Calc spreadsheet
  • Google Sheet (downloadable)
  • Microsoft Excel workbook (secondary, use only if above fail)

Dataset sheets:

  • Howard’s first jail visits: prison name, county, year, month, and day of Howard’s visit, debtors and felons present, with citation to page number of report in Howard’s State of Prisons, 4th ed.

Related datasets:

  • prisoners in England and Wales about 1780
  • estimates of debtor-prisoners in England before 1775
  • historical county names and scope in England and Wales
