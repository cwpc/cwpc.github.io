Prisoners in England and Wales in 1810


Prisoners totals by individual prisons, by sex and debtors, felons, and petty offenders, with prisoners in hulks and in military prisons, from James Neild’s State of the Prisons (1812).

Get dataset in form/version:
  • Google Sheet published to web (fast, resilient)
  • Microsoft Excel workbook (primary version)
  • Apache OpenOffice Calc spreadsheet
  • Google Sheet (downloadable)
  • Microsoft Excel workbook (secondary, use only if above fail)

Dataset sheets:

  • summary: prisoner totals for England and Wales by sex and by debtors, felons, and petty offenders, with prisoners in hulks and in military
  • sex ratio – non-debtors: detailing estimation of sex ratio for non-debtor prisoners
  • prison population: Neild’s prison population figures (debtors, felons, petty offenders and undifferentiated prisoners) for individual prisons, with county, city, and prison-type attributes for each prison
