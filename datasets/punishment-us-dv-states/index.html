II. Gender Imprisonment C. Domestic Violence 2. Criminal Justice

Restraining Orders and Domestic Violence by U.S. States


Domestic-violence restraining orders, along with statistics on arrests for domestic violence and attributes of incidents, with historical data where available, state-published data by U.S. states.

Get dataset in form/version:
  • Google Sheet published to web (fast, resilient)
  • Microsoft Excel workbook (primary version)
  • Apache OpenOffice Calc spreadsheet
  • Google Sheet (downloadable)
  • Microsoft Excel workbook (secondary, use only if above fail)

Dataset sheets:

  • {sheets by state postal code}: restraining order and other domestic violence statistics available for that state from state-specific sources
  • DV sex ratios: sex ratios of victims, offenders, and arrested persons in police-reported domestic violence incidents, compiled from data in state-specific sheets
  • Sweden: restraining orders in Sweden in 2002, with calculation of restraining orders per 1000 adults
  • misc data: additional sources on prevalence of restraining orders
  • US pop 2000-2010: by state, for state-specific prevalence estimates

Related datasets:

  • restraining orders and domestic violence in the U.S.
  • arrests for domestic violence among arrests for all violence in U.S.
  • punishment prevalence yearly by sex in the U.S. from 1850
