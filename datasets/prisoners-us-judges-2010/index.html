Prisoner and Judge Sex Ratios in U.S. by State, 2010


Prisoners and judges by sex and by state in U.S. in 2010, with sex ratios. Judge counts are for judges serving on state courts of general jurisdiction, the courts of first impression for most criminal cases

Get dataset in form/version:
  • Google Sheet published to web (fast, resilient)
  • Microsoft Excel workbook (primary version)
  • Apache OpenOffice Calc spreadsheet
  • Google Sheet (downloadable)
  • Microsoft Excel workbook (secondary, use only if above fail)

Dataset sheets:

  • judges, prisoners: counts of judges and prisoners by sex and by state about 2010. The judge counts are from Forster-Long’s Gender Diversity Survey, in The American Bench: Judges of the Nation. Prisoners counts are from the national census of 2010.

Related datasets:

  • prisoners in the U.S. from 1900
  • imprisonment prevalence and sex ratio in U.S. from 1850
  • prisoners by state and sex, U.S. 1880-2010
