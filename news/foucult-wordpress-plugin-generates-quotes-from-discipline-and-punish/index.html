Foucult WordPress Plugin
Generates Quotes from Discipline and Punish

Michel Foucault’s book Discipline and Punish: The Birth of the Prison has had enormous influence on intellectual life. When activated, the Foucult WordPress plugin honors Foucault’s influence by adding a randomly chosen line adapted from Discipline and Punish to every WordPress admin page instance. The Foucult plugin serves to inspire thinkers building new intellectual life online with WordPress.

The Foucult plugin was adapted from Matt Mullenweg’s pioneering Hello Dolly plugin. Download the Foucult plugin and add it to your WordPress installation.

The Internet knowledge revolution is just beginning. Go big with WordPress!
